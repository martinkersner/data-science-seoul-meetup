How to join us

Seoul AI Hackathon allows you to participate either as a single participant or in a team. In case you want to participate in a team, all members (not more than 2 is recommended) have to RSVP to this event through meetup.com. If you want to search for potential team members, the best way is through our slack.
Before joining slack you have to ask for invitation at this page.
